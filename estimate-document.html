Protection Plans & Liability

Important Liability Notice

Unless Full Value Protection is separately requested and confirmed in writing before transportation begins, this shipment will be transported under Released Value Protection only.

Released Value Protection limits carrier liability to $0.60 per pound per article, subject to applicable transportation rules, exclusions, and service terms.

Optional Full Value Protection

Full Value Protection is not included in this estimate unless separately requested and confirmed in writing before pickup.

If available, it may be purchased for an additional charge of 2.5%-5% of the declared market value, plus a $15 protection processing fee. Higher rates may apply to mirrors, chandeliers, paintings, electronics, antiques, custom, fragile, or non-repairable items.
