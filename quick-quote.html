Purpose

Quick Quote is used for a fast preliminary price range when the customer has not provided all details needed for a full estimate.

Recommended Workflow

  • Select pickup and delivery zones.
  • Choose an item from References Item Catalog or use Custom.
  • Enter approximate volume, weight, and quantity.
  • Use Copy for repeated items and Clear to reset a row.
  • Review Price Drivers before giving the customer a range.
  • Convert to Full Quote when the lead is ready for a final estimate.

Built-in Assumptions

  • Default crew is 2 people.
  • Volume is rounded up to the next whole cu ft.
  • Vehicle is selected automatically by volume and weight.
  • Item Catalog fills common volume, weight, and handling flags.

Price Drivers

  • Route distance and zones.
  • Rounded shipment volume.
  • Weight and selected vehicle.
  • Default crew and delivery priority.
  • Packaging or handling assumptions from Item Catalog.

Important Limitation

Quick Quote is not a final customer estimate. Use it to decide whether the lead should move into a Full Quote with exact addresses, access conditions, item details, protection plan, storage, and final customer-facing estimate generation.
