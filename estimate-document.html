Protection Plans & Liability Coverage

Important Notice Regarding Carrier Liability

Unless Full Value Protection is separately requested and confirmed in writing before transportation begins, the shipment will be transported under Released Value Protection only.

Released Value Protection

Released Value Protection is included in this transportation estimate at no additional charge. Under Released Value Protection, carrier liability is limited to $0.60 per pound per article, subject to applicable transportation regulations, carrier tariff provisions, service terms, exclusions and limitations.

Released Value Protection does not provide reimbursement based on declared value, replacement value, market value, sentimental value, collectible value, antique value, or the cost of repair unless otherwise required by applicable law.

Full Value Protection

Full Value Protection may be purchased separately before transportation begins. Full Value Protection is not included in the transportation cost unless it is expressly requested by the customer and confirmed in writing by Zaberman before pickup.

Full Value Protection is generally available for an additional charge equal to 2.5%–5% of the declared market value of the shipment, plus a $15 insurance processing fee.

A 5% protection rate may apply to high-risk, fragile, non-repairable, antique, collectible, custom, electronic, artwork, mirror, chandelier, painting, or otherwise irreplaceable items.
